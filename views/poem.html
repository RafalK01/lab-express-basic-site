Doggo Poem

Go to homepage About this page About this page


"Humans and dogs, we go way back,
Our love for them is never out of whack.
They bark, they wag, they lick our faces,
And we give them treats and endless embraces.

We dress them up in silly clothes,
And take them out to strike a pose.
We make them Instagram famous,
And watch as their followers amass.

We talk to them like they're human,
And give them nicknames that are truly uncommon.
We let them take over our beds,
And even let them rest their heads.

We throw them birthday parties and cake,
And watch them gobble it down, no mistake.
We spend a fortune on their toys,
And clean up after their endless ploys.

We let them be our therapists and healers,
And never question their opinions or feelers.
We love our dogs, more than we love ourselves,
And for that, we'll never apologize or shelve.

So here's to our furry friends and loyal companions,
Who always bring us joy and endless celebrations.
We love you, our dogs, more than words can say,
And promise to cherish you, each and every day.
"
